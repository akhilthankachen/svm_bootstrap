FACILITIES

Our school is situated in 3acrs of land faraway from the din and bustle of the city though it is a city school. The classrooms are spacies as specified by the CBSE.

Mastering the 3R’s was the main objectives of learning in the ancient time.

Much water has flown under the bridge and rapid changes were brought in the layman’s everyday life. Systematic training of the pupil from infancy brings children to the new world of learning. With that intention. We have started the KG section with most modern amenities for learning through playing.

Learning by doing is the most effective means of securing knowledge. We have provided the labs for different subjects,

Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Computer Science and Mathematics with all facilities. Audio visual Lab with OHP, Slide Projector, LCD Monitor and all other accessories boost the task of learning.

A well setup library under the strict guidance of a well qualified Librarian with internet facility is provided.

The use of library is made mandatory to class I to XII with varied range.
